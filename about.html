About Our Church

Serving our community with faith, love, and dedication since our foundation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a beacon of spiritual light in our community, dedicated to spreading God's Word, building strong and faithful families, and serving those in need with compassion and love.

We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where every person feels valued, welcomed, and empowered to grow in their faith and contribute to our community.

Our Vision

Faith Foundation Family Church envisioned creating a strong community, transcending boundaries, and spreading the Savior's gospel throughout the world. It was a call to unite believers under one umbrella, fostering an environment where faith could flourish, transforming lives, and impacting the world with the transformative power of the Gospel.

Our History

In the heart of Mukuru Kwa Njenga slums, a beacon of faith emerged on January 8th, 2023 - Faith Foundation Family Church. Founded by the visionary leader, Presiding Bishop Fredrick Ondu, this ministry was born out of a divine calling to unite the diverse body of Christ into a familial bond of worship and fellowship.

Presiding Bishop Fredrick Ondu, driven by a profound vision, established the Faith Foundation Family Church in 2022. His inspiration, drawn from Hebrews 11:6 - "And without faith it is impossible to please God, because anyone who comes to him must believe that he exists and that he rewards those who earnestly seek him," became the guiding light for the community that would soon gather around this newfound spiritual haven.

The Church's headquarters, situated amidst the challenges of Mukuru Kwa Njenga slums, became a testament to its commitment to reaching out to those in need and creating a strong community bound by faith. The biblical statement served as the cornerstone, emphasizing the importance of faith in pleasing God and the reward awaiting those who earnestly seek Him.

Our Core Values

Faith First

We believe that faith in God is the foundation of everything we do. It guides our decisions, empowers our ministry, and transforms lives.

Strong Families

Family is at the heart of our mission. We support and strengthen families through biblical teaching, counseling, and community programs.

Community Service

We are committed to serving our community and making a positive impact. Love in action is how we express our faith.

Inclusivity

Everyone is welcome here. We celebrate diversity and create a welcoming environment for people from all backgrounds and walks of life.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do. From our worship services to our outreach programs, we aim to honor God with our best.

Growth & Learning

We believe in continuous spiritual growth and learning. We provide resources and opportunities for members to deepen their faith journey.
