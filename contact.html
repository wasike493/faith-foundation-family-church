Frequently Asked Questions

What time are the Sunday services?

Our main Sunday service begins at 10:00 AM and typically lasts about 90 minutes. We also have a prayer time 30 minutes before the service for those who wish to arrive early.

Do I need to be a member to attend services?

Not at all! Everyone is welcome to attend our services. We have no membership requirements and encourage people from all backgrounds to join us.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We offer Sunday school classes during service times, youth groups, and special children's events throughout the year, including our annual VBS camp.

How can I get more involved in the church?

We have many ministries and volunteer opportunities available. Contact us to learn about serving in areas such as ushering, music, children's ministry, outreach, and more.

Do you offer online services?

Yes! We stream our Sunday services live on our website and YouTube channel. You can join us from anywhere in the world.

How do I submit a prayer request?

You can submit prayer requests through our contact form above, call our prayer line, or email us your request. All prayer requests are handled with confidentiality and compassion.
